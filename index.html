Intro

The ATS token is used for a variety of different purposes throughout the Harmony ecosystem.

Atlantis (ATS) is an HRC-20 token running on the Harmony network and the currency of ONE. The token max supply is 481M ATSs, with a potential 230M ATSs allocated for burn at a later stage. The ATS token can be held in the farm wallet or externally in MetaMask, staked in both cases at 44% apy (see staking). To learn even more, visit the About ATS page.

The Harmony faucet is our way of giving back to the community and getting new Harmony enthusiasts to engage with the currency. To receive your Harmony from our faucet, all you have to do is win a couple of Crypto Padi USD to get rewarded with ATS. Once you have not more than 20 ATS, you can convert your reward to ONE with our faucet. Please note that the upper limit is currently 20 ATS. If you need to swap more ATS, we recommend using Padi Swap.

About ATS

Atlantis (ATS) is an HRC-20 token running on the Harmony network and the currency of Crypto Atlantis.

The token max supply is 481M ATSs, with a potential 230M ATSs allocated for burn at a later stage. The ATS token can be held in the farm wallet or externally in MetaMask.

It can also be staked, either at a fixed 20% APY or variable, through Padi Farms.
